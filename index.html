About the College

Kharagpur College, established in 1949, is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in West Bengal, India. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in arts, science, and commerce. The college is known for its academic excellence, experienced faculty, and vibrant campus life. It is affiliated with Vidyasagar University and has a strong emphasis on research and extracurricular activities.
